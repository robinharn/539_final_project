About

I'm a student at the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) studying UI/UX design. I have a background in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and C#, and I'm always looking to add new tools and languages to my repertoire. Outside of work and the classroom, I love playing board games, volleyball, and tennis.

I help digitize classic board games by creating online implementations of them on virtualtabletop.io, which is a free, open-source, web-based platform. I primarily work in Javascript and CSS to bring these implementations to life. You can find some of my latest work below!
